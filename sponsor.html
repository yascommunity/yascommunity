Partner with Us or Sponsor Us

Our community is built around open-source projects and teaching people how to code, making it the perfect platform for tech companies to support us. Not only do we have a large presence on Discord, but we also have a popular YouTube channel and a growing following on Instagram and Twitter.

By partnering with or sponsoring our community, your brand will have the opportunity to reach a highly engaged and tech-savvy audience. Additionally, as a community that values education and personal development, our members will appreciate the support and resources provided by a tech company partner or sponsor.

Whether you are a small startup or a large corporation, we believe that there is value in supporting and investing in the next generation of tech professionals, and we would be honored to have your support as a partner or sponsor.
