Be Part of the Team

Why You Should Join Us

The YasCommunity is a vibrant and expanding group of individuals who are passionate about technology. Initially founded as a way to assist those interested in open-source Sneaker Monitors, the community has now grown to encompass a much wider range of interests and activities. Our goal is to continue providing valuable resources and support to tech enthusiasts in the future.

There are many ways to get involved with the YasCommunity, including managing our social media accounts, organizing events on our Discord server, and contributing projects and content. If you have your own ideas for how to enhance the community, we welcome your participation and input.

Currently, all positions within the YasCommunity are voluntary. However, as we explore new opportunities for generating revenue, we hope to be able to convert some of these roles into paid positions in the future.

If you are interested in joining our team, please fill out the Google Form provided.
